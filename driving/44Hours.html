Refreshed

Had another lesson since I hadn’t driven in a while and I just wanted to be behind the wheel to be honest.
The drive was overall very smooth - the only issue I did was about 10 mins into my lesson.
There was a roundabout and I almost pulled out on someone as their road position was hard to see.
Apart from that I did all the parking manoeuvres first time, the mock test I did didn’t have any serious or dangerous faults.
Emergency stop I did well, and I also followed road signs.
I guess this post is to say keep going!
For people who are still learning, people who are doing refresher lessons, whatever it may be just know it will all be worth it.
Don’t take any risks or chances - multiple times throughout I held back or waited even if I “could have” gone and it wasn’t a minor.
I don’t know if you have to say it, but I also said out loud “I’m gonna wait until the X colour car has gone”.
Or “I’m giving way because I’m not fitting through that gap”.
I only do commentary driving when I absolutely need to, it’s just my personality.
