Mistake Master

Had a really good drive according to my driving instructor.
but dang I really make some of the most stupid mistakes and decisions ever…
Saw an oncoming car and there was easily enough time to get into a space, but I decided to go ahead.
Did that twice and both times I was like “why the hell did I do that then?!”
Such a dumb decision because every other time today I had good planning and pulled in when necessary.
Roundabouts I still need to get better at, by the end I was a bit more confident at taking opportunities but yeah.
Did every parking manoeuvre first time except the reverse park. Had to do that multiple times.
Eventually got it in but it was embarrassingly painful.
On the way back we were joining into a motorway…and I didn’t check my mirrors.
So the van had to slow down a bit. Think I was too focused on getting my speed up.
I completely missed to check them. But I won’t do that again.
I guess this is to say…messing up is the best teacher.
As long as you learn from it!
And that even if you’re nearing your driving assessment day, you are still going to be making mistakes and bad decisions.
