100 Days Since I Started Learning - February 4th 2024

I guess this is a nice reflection from where I started.
Had a lesson today, and after a shaky start I got my ass into gear for a decent drive.
Instructor still had to use the dual controls a handful of times.
I was going a bit too quick when approaching roundabouts.
But by the end of the lesson I was doing them safely and at the appropriate speed.
Did the emergency stop procedure as well for the first time.
Nailed it twice in a row but I’m thinking that’s beginner’s luck tbh.
18 hours of driving lessons, approximately 12 hours of those behind the wheel.
Definitely making progress but I just need to get my head in the game from the moment I get behind the wheel.
Positioning is also SO much better, very rarely am I told to move to the right and it’s just very minor adjustments.
Still need to be aware of not hitting the kerb and pre emptive turning at junctions to go left.
It feels unnatural in a way, turning first then checking second but whatever.
I think it’s natural to think “why did I do that / why did I not do that” constantly and “I’m never gonna pass”.
But we gotta keep going! 😊
And remember why we are actually doing this.
And I’m binge watching driving videos instead of anime :P
